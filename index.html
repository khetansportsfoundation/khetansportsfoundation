KHETAN SPORTS FOUNDATION


Khetan Sports Foundation (KSF) is a non-profit organization committed for transforming grassroots sports in Nepal. By supporting young athletes, especially from underrepresented communities, KSF promotes equal opportunity and inclusive growth. Through partnerships, training, and expert-led programs, we enable athletes to thrive on and off the field. Our mission is to build a strong sporting ecosystem driven by passion, discipline, and innovation.

AREAS OF FOCUS

We have directed our initiatives towards creating an impact in the sports ecosystem.

Grassroots Development

We are working with academies to support grassroots athletes through targeted interventions that foster their holistic growth and help them achieve their sporting aspirations.

Sports Science

We connect partner academies with sports science experts including coaches, nutritionists, and sports psychologists through dynamic workshops to enhance athlete performance.

Women in Sports

Empowering women in sports by breaking barriers of access and stereotypes across Nepal.

Sports Collaborations

We have collaborated with various National Sports Federations, offering support in sports science, equipment, expertise, technology, and research.





Our “Women in Sports” initiative supports girls and women across communities, fostering inclusivity and strength.
